This questionnaire will help you better understand ADHD symptoms. Through a series of questions, you will discover different aspects of this disorder and receive detailed explanations for each answer.

En plus d'évaluer les symptômes classiques, ce test aborde également les aspects moins connus du TDAH comme les stratégies d'adaptation, l'impact émotionnel et les forces potentielles liées à ce fonctionnement neurologique différent. In addition to assessing classic symptoms, this test also addresses lesser-known aspects of ADHD such as coping strategies, emotional impact, and potential strengths related to this different neurological functioning.
